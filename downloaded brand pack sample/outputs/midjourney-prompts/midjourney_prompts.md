## Tips for Using These Prompts

1. **Customize:** Feel free to adjust prompts based on your specific needs
2. **Iterate:** Use `--seed` to maintain consistency across variations
3. **Aspect Ratios:** Adjust `--ar` based on your use case (16:9 for web, 9:16 for mobile, 1:1 for social)
4. **Style Consistency:** Use the same style references across prompts for brand coherence
5. **Version:** These prompts are optimized for Midjourney v6+
