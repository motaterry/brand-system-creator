## Logo Design Guidelines

### Considerations for Rota do Peso

#### Wordmark vs. Symbol

**Wordmark Advantages:**
- Immediate name recognition
- Better for new or lesser-known brands
- No symbol interpretation needed
- Works well for Rota do Peso if name is distinctive

**Symbol Advantages:**
- More compact for small applications
- Can work without text (app icons)
- More versatile across languages
- Stronger visual memory

**Recommendation:** Can work as wordmark, symbol optional

#### Scalability Requirements

**Must work at:**
- Favicon size (16×16px, 32×32px)
- Mobile app icon (1024×1024px)
- Social media profile (various sizes)
- Business card (small print)
- Billboard or large format

**Design accordingly:**
- Avoid thin lines that disappear at small sizes
- Ensure details are meaningful at all scales
- Test in monochrome (will it be recognizable?)

#### Color Flexibility

**Develop versions:**
1. **Full color:** Primary brand colors
2. **Monochrome:** Single color (usually black)
3. **Reverse:** For dark backgrounds (usually white)
4. **Grayscale:** For B&W reproduction

#### Clear Space

Define minimum clear space around logo:
- Typically equal to height of a key letter
- Keeps logo from visual clutter
- Ensures proper breathing room

#### Incorrect Usage Examples

Document what NOT to do:
- Don't stretch or distort
- Don't change colors without approval
- Don't rotate or skew
- Don't add effects (shadows, glows, etc.)
- Don't place on busy backgrounds
- Don't place colors that fail contrast tests

## Application Examples

### Favicon (16×16px, 32×32px)

**Considerations:**
- Extremely limited space
- Often monochrome or very limited color
- Must be recognizable at tiny size
- Consider: Single letter, simple symbol, or monogram

**For Rota do Peso:**
Use "R" as monogram or simplified symbol

### App Icon (iOS: 1024×1024px)

**Requirements:**
- No transparency (solid background)
- Fills square format
- Rounded corners applied by OS
- Must be distinctive in app drawer
- Should maintain brand recognition

**Approach:**
- Symbol-based (if available)
- Lettermark on brand color background
- Avoid detailed illustrations

### Website Header

**Horizontal lockup:**
- Typically wider than tall
- Include tagline if helpful: "Compare clínicas, escolha com segurança."
- Consider dark and light versions for different page styles

**Responsive behavior:**
- Full logo on desktop
- Simplified or symbol-only on mobile
- Maintain recognition across sizes

### Business Card

**Dimensions:** 3.5" × 2" (89mm × 51mm)

**Placement:**
- Front: Main logo placement
- Back: Optional secondary placement or pattern
- Ensure legibility at card size
- Consider vertical or horizontal orientation

### Social Media

**Profile Pictures (circular crop):**
- Design must work in circle
- Centered, balanced composition
- Simple enough for small display

**Cover/Banner Images:**
- Opportunity for full brand expression
- Can include tagline, visuals, messaging
- Update seasonally or for campaigns

### Email Signature

**Size:** ~150-200px wide maximum
**Format:** PNG with transparency (or solid background)
**Consideration:** Must look good on various email clients

### Presentation Materials

**PowerPoint/Keynote:**
- Corner placement (typically top-left or bottom-right)
- Small but legible
- Monochrome version often best for not competing with content

### Merchandise

**T-shirts, mugs, pens, etc.:**
- Consider single-color versions (screen printing cost)
- Test at actual production sizes
- Ensure design translates to different materials
- Embroidery requires simplified version

## Next Steps

### Professional Design Process

1. **Select Direction:** Choose 1-2 concepts to explore
2. **Hire Designer:** Work with professional logo designer
3. **Iterations:** Expect 2-3 rounds of refinement
4. **Finalize:** Get all file formats and color versions
5. **Guidelines:** Create comprehensive brand guidelines

### File Formats Needed

**Vector:**
- AI (Adobe Illustrator source)
- EPS (universal vector)
- SVG (web vector)
- PDF (print and sharing)

**Raster:**
- PNG with transparency (web, various sizes)
- JPG on white background (various sizes)
- Favicon formats (ICO, multiple sizes)

### Trademark Considerations

- Search existing trademarks in your industry
- Consider trademark registration
- Ensure logo is distinctive enough to protect
- Consult intellectual property attorney
